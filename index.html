Get into tech course coverd a range of topics in order to help students become a software developer. Some software developers can be classed as 'i' type as they have a deep knowledge in one area. Other software developers are classed as "t" with a deep knoweldge in one area and a slightly wider knowledge in other languages. Finally an M shaped developer has both a wide and deep knowledge of a variety of technologies.
